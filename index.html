Our Story

Bringing Malaysian Heritage to Your Table

Founded in 2010, Rasa Malaysia Bakery began as a small family business in Kuala Lumpur with a passion for preserving traditional Malaysian baking techniques while innovating with modern flavors.

Our master bakers combine generations-old recipes with the finest local ingredients to create authentic Malaysian pastries, cakes, and breads that tell the story of our rich culinary heritage.
